From Vision to Reality: University College London Hospital

From vision to reality:
University College London Hospital
From vision to reality:
University College London Hospital Capgemini worked closely with University College
London Hospital to develop and implement a solution
that reflects a key tenet of Every Child Matters: to bring
together the organisations involved with providing
services to children for the benefits of every child. In
this case, it is disparate medical centres that are now
sharing information and provding young patients with a
joined up approach so that they receive information
and support in a timely manner.
